	<h2>Apply Today!</h2>

	<p>The Community Service Graduation Pin honors students who have demonstrated exceptional dedication to supporting and empowering our local Merced community. This recognition is awarded to those who have embraced experiential opportunities, allowing these experiences to shape them into exemplary Bobcats.</p>

	<p>These students have gone above and beyond, applying their knowledge and skills to serve the Merced community while gaining valuable real-world experience.</p>

	<p>The Community Engagement Center and UC Merced commend our students who excel in learning through hands-on experiences. By engaging in real-world service opportunities, our students develop as individuals and leaders, making a lasting impact on the lives of those they serve.</p>

	<p>The Community Service Graduation Pin celebrates these outstanding students and their commitment to making Merced a better place for everyone.</p>

	<h4>Eligibility Requirements</h4>

	<p>Students must meet <strong>one</strong> of the following criteria:</p>

	<ul>
		<li><strong>Participation in a Long Term Service Project &ndash;</strong> Volunteers must stay for the entire duration of the program. To find eligible Long Term Programs please click <a href="https://cec.ucmerced.edu/impact-initiatives" target="_blank">HERE</a>.</li>
		<li><strong>30 Approved Catlife hours</strong> of Community Engagement Center Events &ndash; These must be approved prior to submitting an application.</li>
		<li><strong>30 Approved Catlife hours</strong> of service through UC Merced Clubs/Orgs &ndash; These must be approved prior to submitting an application.</li>
	</ul>

	<h2>Why Should I Apply?</h2>

	<ul>
		<li>Gain valuable experiential opportunities to build your skills.</li>
		<li>Leave a lasting legacy and make a strong impact in your community.</li>
		<li>Receive recognition for your hard work and dedication.</li>
	</ul>

	<h2>How Do I Apply?</h2>

	<p>When students are approaching graduation they will receive an email with instructions on how to apply as well as the required application form. All graduating students should check their UC Merced email inbox for this message.</p>

	<h2>Have Questions?</h2>
