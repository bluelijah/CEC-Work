<h3 class="head-line">For UC Merced Students, Clubs, and Organizations</h3>

<p>If you are interested in learning more about staying engaged in the community remotely, try attending our monthly Public Service Announcements! Formerly known as Virtual Service Talks, we welcome all UC Merced students and would love to help you find community service opportunities. Click on one of the buttons below for more information or watch our past recordings on our <a href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCHsVlg3u2ABqZAo4552q9lQ?view_as=subscriber">YouTube channel</a>, also shown below!&nbsp;</p>

<p>Join us on <strong>Wednesday, September 3rd</strong><strong>&nbsp;at 5:00PM at COB 2 265&nbsp;</strong>to learn more about upcoming one-time service opportunities, longer programs, and community engagement events!&nbsp;</p>
