<div class="award-container">
    <h2>Are you a member of a registered student club or organization on campus?</h2>

    <p>If so, you are cordially invited to apply for the University Friends Circle Community Service Organization Award!</p>

    <p>This award recognizes student clubs or organizations for exemplary service in the community surrounding UC Merced. The University Friends Circle may present multiple organizations with a <strong>$1,000 award</strong> to support continued community service efforts.</p>

    <div class="deadline-box" role="note">
        <p><strong>Deadline for submissions:</strong> Monday, March 9, 2026 at 11:59 PM</p>
        <p><strong>Finalist notification:</strong> Organizations selected for an interview will be notified by March 31st</p>
        <p><strong>Interviews:</strong> Wednesday, April 8 and Thursday, April 9 in KL 184D (Student Career Center Conference Room). At least 3 members of the organization must be present for the interview.</p>
    </div>

    <h2>Application Details</h2>

    <p>The application will ask for nominee/nominator information and responses for 6 short-answer prompts. Only information provided through the form will be used to select finalists. <strong>Self-nominations are allowed and encouraged!</strong></p>

    <p><strong>Important:</strong> Each organization must submit only <strong>one application</strong>. If your organization chooses to apply, please select <strong>one member to serve as the official representative</strong> and submit the application on behalf of the entire organization. Multiple organizations may receive this award.</p>

    <h2>Nominated organization must:</h2>

    <ul>
        <li>Be in good standing with the university</li>
        <li>If selected as a finalist, agree to an interview with the UFC Scholarship Committee (typically held in April). At least 3 members of the organization must be present for the interview.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Interview Preparation</h2>

    <p>Organizations invited to the interview should prepare with the following information:</p>

    <ul>
        <li>What is your organization's mission and values? How did the service your organization provide align with your mission and values?</li>
        <li>How is the organization planning to improve (or build on) its current service initiatives?</li>
    </ul>

    <p>We encourage the members attending the interview to dress business casual! A good first impression goes a long way!</p>
