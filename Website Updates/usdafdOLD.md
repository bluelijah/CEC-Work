<p>USDA&#39;s&nbsp;food&nbsp;distribution&nbsp;programs strengthen the nutrition safety net through the&nbsp;distribution&nbsp;of&nbsp;USDA&nbsp;Foods and other nutrition assistance to children, low-income families, emergency feeding programs, and the elderly.&nbsp;This service is<strong>&nbsp;open to all students, staff, and community members</strong>&nbsp;in need of food assistance!&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>

<p><strong><em>Food distributions no longer occur on a regular schedule, please check our calendar for more details such as time and location. </em></strong><strong><em>Transportation will NOT be provided.</em></strong>&nbsp;For questions or accommodations, please contact the Community Engagement Center at <strong>communityservice@ucmerced.edu.</strong></p>
