<h2><strong>We are now Accepting Nominations for the 2025&nbsp;University Friends Circle - Community Service Organization of the Year Award!</strong></h2>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<h3><strong>If you are a member of a registered student club or organization on campus, you are cordially invited to apply for the 2025&nbsp;University Friends Circle Community Service Organization Award.</strong></h3>

<p>This award recognizes a student club or organization for exemplary service in the community surrounding UC Merced. The University Friends Circle will present the recipient with a <strong>$1,000 award</strong> to support continued community service efforts.</p>

<p><strong>Nominations are due by 11:59pm on Friday, March 7, 2025</strong></p>

<h4><span style="color:red;"><strong>**Only 1 Self-Nomination per student organization**</strong></span></h4>

<p>This application for the University Friends Circle Community Service Organization Award will ask for nominee/nominator information and for responses for 6 short answer prompts. Only information provided through this form will be used to select finalists. <strong>Self-nominations are allowed and encouraged!</strong></p>

<h2><strong>Nominees must:</strong></h2>

<ul>
	<li>Be in good judicial standing with the university</li>
	<li>Agree to an interview with the UFC Scholarship Committee on <strong>Wednesday, April 9</strong>&nbsp;or <strong>Thursday, April 10</strong>&nbsp;if selected as a finalist. (Only 2 e-board members are needed for the interview.)</li>
</ul>
