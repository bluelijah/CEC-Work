<h2><strong>Attention all Bobcats!!</strong></h2>

<p>We are proud to introduce an exciting long-term volunteer program in partnership with the Merced County Office of Education! This program offers a unique opportunity to make a meaningful difference by supporting Merced residents in their pursuit of educational success. Volunteers will play a crucial role in assisting students striving for their high school diplomas or helping younger learners enhance their proficiency in a second language.</p>

<p>By being a part of this program, you&rsquo;ll provide essential classroom support, help students reach their academic goals, and gain valuable hands-on experience in education and community service. This is an excellent opportunity for those looking to make a lasting impact while building skills in teaching and mentorship. <em><strong>This program qualified for the Service Experiential Graduation Pin and Catlife Service Hours. At the completion of the program, you will receive a Linked.In endorsement from CEC staff.&nbsp;</strong></em></p>

<h4><strong>Volunteer Requirements</strong></h4>

<ul>
	<li>Attend one info session&nbsp;</li>
	<li>MCOE background check (paid by MCOE)&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Proficiency of English and Spanish <em>only&nbsp;if volunteering in the DLI program &nbsp;</em></li>
	<li>Must attend in a reoccurring basis to the selected site - can only miss 3 days.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Commitment: Long-term availability with flexible shifts based on school schedules.</li>
</ul>
