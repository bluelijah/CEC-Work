<h2 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Welcome Prospective Bobcats!&nbsp;</strong></h2>

<h2>Start Your Journey as a Changemaker &mdash; Right from Your First Year!</h2>

<p>Congratulations on your upcoming adventure at UC Merced! Located in the heart of campus in the Kolligian Library, the&nbsp;Margo F. Souza Student Leadership Center&nbsp;and the&nbsp;Community Engagement Center (CEC)&nbsp;are here to connect you with powerful leadership and service opportunities that will shape your college experience and beyond.</p>

<p>If you&rsquo;re passionate about making a difference and developing your leadership potential, the&nbsp;Leadership &amp; Service Living Learning Community (LLC)&nbsp;is your perfect starting point! <strong>Exclusively for&nbsp;first-year students</strong>, this dynamic program brings together 30 driven individuals to live, learn, and lead together.</p>

<p><em><strong>As a member of the Leadership &amp; Service LLC, you&rsquo;ll:</strong></em></p>

<ul>
	<li>Explore real-world leadership concepts through hands-on activities and service projects</li>
	<li>Build a supportive network of like-minded peers, mentors, and professional staff</li>
	<li>Gain resume-boosting experience and transferable skills through experiential learning</li>
	<li>Receive personalized support and guidance throughout your first year</li>
	<li>Get priority access to special events and opportunities</li>
	<li>Request strong letters of recommendation from staff who know you well</li>
</ul>

<p>This is more than a place to live &mdash; it&#39;s a launching pad for your personal growth and impact. Don&rsquo;t miss your chance to become part of a close-knit community that empowers you to lead, serve, and shine from day one.</p>

<h2>Requirements for LLC Members</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Students are required to attend weekly Friday meetings from 1 - 2:30 PM.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Students must schedule a check-in with their LLC advisor at least once a semester.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Students must participate in the Leadership and Service LLC retreat in the fall and spring celebration.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Participants must commit to community engagement participation in both fall and spring semesters.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Students must attend one of three Valley Adventure and Service trips in the spring semester.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Participants must be in good academic and student conduct standing throughout the duration of the experience.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Only students who have completed the LLC application and signed their housing contract by May 23, 2025&nbsp;will be considered for the Leadership and Service LLC.&nbsp;</strong></p>
