<p class="trickortreat-header">Join Our Downtown Merced Trick-or-Treat Street Volunteer Team!</p>

<p>We are looking for <span class="highlight">dedicated and enthusiastic volunteers</span> to help with this year&#39;s Downtown Merced Trick-or-Treat Street on <span class="highlight">Saturday, October 26th</span> in <span class="highlight">Downtown Merced</span>.</p>

<div class="schedule-box"><span class="highlight">Event Schedule:</span><br />
	Volunteer Check-In &amp; Orientation: 1:45 PM<br />
	Setup &amp; Duties Begin: 2:00 PM<br />
	Event: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM</div>

<div class="volunteer-box">
	<h2>Volunteer Opportunities</h2>

	<p>Help make this Trick-or-Treat Street safe and fun! Volunteer duties include:</p>

	<ul>
		<li><span class="highlight">Event Setup &amp; Decorating</span> &ndash; Help create a festive atmosphere for the event.</li>
		<li><span class="highlight">Signage Distribution</span> &ndash; Deliver Treat Stop flyers to participating businesses.</li>
		<li><span class="highlight">Candy Runners</span> &ndash; Walk candy from homebase to businesses that need replenishment.</li>
		<li><span class="highlight">Costume Contest Assistance</span> &ndash; Help manage costume contest entries.</li>
		<li><span class="highlight">Crossing Assistance</span> &ndash; Help trick-or-treaters safely cross streets at crosswalks.</li>
		<li><span class="highlight">General Support</span> &ndash; Assist with other duties as needed throughout the event.</li>
	</ul>

	<p style="text-align: center; margin-top: 15px;"><span class="highlight">Check-In Location:</span> Abracadabra (set up next door to Luna Luna 209/Abracadabra, in the space formerly Modern Living)</p>
</div>
